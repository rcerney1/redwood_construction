About Us

Engineering Reliability. Building the Bay Area.

Redwood Engineering Construction is a licensed and insured contractor specializing in excavation, grading, underground utilities, drainage, parks, and structural repair throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. Since 2015, we’ve built a reputation for completing both public and private projects on time, on budget, and to the highest standards of quality and safety.
