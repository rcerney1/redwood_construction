Professional Landslide & Slope Repair

Since 2015, Redwood Engineering Construction has delivered trusted structural support and geotechnical solutions throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. We specialize in repairing failing slopes, retaining walls, and foundations for residential, commercial, and public sector clients.

Geotechnical & Structural Expertise

Our team provides comprehensive landslide remediation and slope stabilization services — from steep hillside repairs to seismic applications and bluff stabilization. We engineer long-term, code-compliant solutions that match the project site and budget.

Our Services Include

  • Limited access drilling in constrained or sloped areas
  • Helical piers and tieback anchors for foundation support
  • New retaining wall construction and failing wall repair
  • Geotechnical engineering for slope stability
  • Seismic retrofitting and structural reinforcement
  • Environmental enhancement and erosion control

Fully licensed and insured, we’ve earned the trust of municipalities and property owners alike. Redwood Engineering Construction is committed to delivering safe, lasting results that protect your investment and the surrounding landscape.
