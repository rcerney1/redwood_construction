Professional Grading & Excavation Services

Redwood Engineering Construction employs some of the most experienced and hardworking engineers in the San Francisco Bay Area. We provide grading and excavation services for residential, commercial, and government properties alike. Whether you're digging a new swimming pool or clearing land for development, we have the expertise and equipment to get the job done right.

Experienced Excavator Operators

Our team operates a full fleet of powerful machinery including bulldozers, front-end loaders, trenchers, and backhoes. Each operator is thoroughly trained and tested to ensure the safe and efficient use of every piece of equipment. You can trust us to handle your job with precision and care.

On-Site Preparation

Proper site preparation is key to any successful construction project. We begin by clearing the land and grading it for a level foundation. We also perform compression tests as needed to confirm that the ground can support future structures. Our process ensures a stable, code-compliant site every time.

If you’re planning a new build or need excavation work done anywhere in the Bay Area, Redwood Engineering Construction is ready to help. With decades of combined experience and a commitment to quality, we’ll get your project off to a solid start.
