De Anza Park

This Project is rehabilitation of an existing park in Sunnyvale that contained sand and deteriorated resilient surface with play equipment installed in 1993. The old play equipment, sand and surfacing was removed and replaced. An “adventure” playground was created through the use of new rubber surfacing resembling the ocean and green elevated “islands” that serve as play features in and of themselves. The new play equipment insalled reinforces the adventure theme with ample opportunities for climbing, swinging, slideing, spinning and exploring. Engineered wood fiber was also installed to add interest in the park and serve as a cost saving measure.

Grading and drainage improvements were also incorporated into the build out of the rehabiitated park.
